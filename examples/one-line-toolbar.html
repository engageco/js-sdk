About Us
www.mainecampexperience.com is the community of premier overnight summer camps for boys & girls. Maine camps are the gold standard, many 100+ years old welcoming campers for generations. Camps feature crystal clear lakes, pristine natural settings, strong traditions & values, world-class facilities, foremost supervision & safety by well-trained staff, scenic trips & top-notch instruction. Each summer 20,000 kids attend Maine Camps from nearly every US state & 30 countries. Our Campcierge™ assists advisors/families select the right camp. Camps for kids ages 7-17; full (6-8 weeks) & short (2-4 weeks) sessions. English language immersion.
